Law Enforcement & Security

For over 30 years, Soke Shidoshi Steven Williams has trained officers from multiple agencies — including LAPD and LASD — in close-quarter tactics built for the realities of the job, not the mat.

This training is available to departments and private security teams by direct booking, covering control tactics, weapons retention, and defensive response under high-stress conditions.

  • Weapons retentionMaintaining control of a firearm or sidearm under physical contact
