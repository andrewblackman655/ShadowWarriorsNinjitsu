Women's Programs

Focused, practical seminars built around the situations women are statistically most likely to face — not a general self-defense overview, but direct instruction in recognizing threats early and responding decisively.

Sessions are taught by Soke Shidoshi Steven Williams and senior instructors, drawing on the same close-quarter combat curriculum trained by law enforcement and security professionals — adapted for real-world application, not competition.

  • Threat recognition & avoidanceReading a situation before it escalates
  • Close-quarter release & escapeBreaking grabs, holds, and restraints decisively
  • Weapons awarenessRecognizing and responding to armed threats
  • Confidence under stressTraining designed to hold up outside a controlled room

Format

  • Group seminars, booked periodically
  • Private sessions available on request
  • No martial arts experience required
  • Open to all ages 16 and up
